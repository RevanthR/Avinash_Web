Refrigerator Condensors

Raw Materials

1. Single Wall Steel Tube – Ø4, Ø4.76, Ø6, Ø6.35, Wall Thickness - 0.5, 0.6, 0.7
2. Low Carbon Steel wire Ø1.2-1.6
3. Bracket – Steel Plate (SPCC) thickness 0.6-1.5

Structure

A. Flat type wire on tube condenser for domestic refrigerators – (Wire on Tube Condensers)
B. Box type/folded/spiral type of wire on tube condenser used as bottom condensers – (Spiral Condensers)
C. Skin/Internal/Frost-free condensers (Skin Condensers)
D. Anti-Moisture Tubes/Door frame heaters (Flange Heaters)
E. Aluminium fin on aluminium tube spiral condenser (Fin-Type Condensers)

We can produce according to the drawing or sample supplied by clients, also can help the clients design, and produce different specs of condensers.

Performance

1. Surface with powder coating and liquid painting to prevent the corrosion
2. Inner cleanliness which meets the requirements of CFC & R134a cooling systems
3. Compatible with RoHS and REACH

Main Production Process

Tube bending, Welding, fixing of bracket, Painting, Folding, or bending and shaping, Inspection and Packing

Pipe Joint Assemblies

Copper or Zinc coated steel tubes covered in a plastic sleeve for usage in refrigerators.

Plate on Tube Evaporators

Raw Material

Aluminium Plate, Steel tubes

Dimensions

As per consumer requirements

Usage

Plate tube evaporators or sandwich evaporators are attached to the back of the refrigerator freezer’s liner, used for cooling in single and double door refrigerator freezers.

Advantages

Uses aluminium plate for cost saving, more surface area for heat dissipation, has a higher efficiency in exchanging heat and is easier to manufacture.

Tubes

Welded steel tubes are mainly of two types, Single wall steel tube(SWST) or Double wall stell tube(DWST) and have wide variety of uses like :

1.Single wall steel tube with copper coating for external condenser application

2.SWST w inside and outside copper coating for automobile fuel line application

3.SWST w zinc coating and PVC coating for CNG lines in automobiles

4.SWST w zinc coating for skin/internal/ff condenser application

5.DWST with zinc and PVF coating for automobile brake line application

6.DWST with copper coating (dia = 3.18mm) for compressor tube application

Raw Material

IF grade CRCA thickness – 0.50-1mm
